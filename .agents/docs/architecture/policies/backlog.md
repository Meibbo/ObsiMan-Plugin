# Backlog Policy

## Rules

- New ideas outside an initiative start in `work/draft`.
- Initiative work routes through that initiative index.
- Conflicts become explicit backlog or triage items, not hidden notes.
- Completed work records lightweight metrics when requested by the plan.
- Rejected drafts move to archive instead of staying active.

## Read When

- Creating tasks, triaging ideas, or resolving plan/spec conflicts.
- Moving work between draft, initiative, and archive.

## Do Not Read When

- Executing a fully scoped plan slice with no backlog changes.

## Related Decisions

- PKM-AI backlog conflict and verification specs.

## Repair Triggers

- Duplicate active tasks exist.
- Draft work is implemented without promotion or triage.
- Completed items lack outcome or verification notes when required.
